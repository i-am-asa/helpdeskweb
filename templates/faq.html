Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: How do I raise a ticket?

Answer: To raise a ticket, click on the "Raise a Ticket" button in the navbar and follow the instructions on the form.

Question 2: How can I view my previous tickets?

Answer: You can view your previous tickets by clicking on the "View Previous Tickets" button in the navbar.

Question 3: How do I contact support?

Answer: For support, you can use the chat widget in the bottom right corner of the screen.

Question 4: How do I log out?

Answer: You can log out by clicking on the "Logout" button in the navbar.

Question 5: Can I change my password?

Answer: Yes, you can change your password by visiting the "Account Settings" page and following the instructions.

Question 6: How do I update my profile information?

Answer: To update your profile information, go to the "Profile" section and click on the "Edit Profile" button.
